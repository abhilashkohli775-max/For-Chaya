For Chaya

A few honest words from my heart

I’ve thought a lot before writing this.
Not to convince you.
Not to force anything.

Just because keeping everything inside felt heavier than saying it once with honesty.

I know the last one and a half months were difficult.
For you, and for me.

I was overwhelmed, anxious, and emotionally unstable at times.
Instead of understanding you, I reacted from fear.

I know that must have hurt you — and I take responsibility for that.

“Let’s start all over again.
I am not the person I was in the past one and a half months.”

I’ve slowed down.
I’ve reflected.
I’ve understood where I went wrong and why.

I’m calmer now.
More aware of my emotions.
More capable of listening instead of reacting.

What I felt for you was real.
It wasn’t habit.
It wasn’t convenience.

It was genuine care.

You mattered to me then.
You matter to me now.

This page is not a request or a demand.
It’s not meant to pressure you into anything.

I just wanted to leave behind honesty instead of silence.

If there is ever space to talk calmly, I’m here.
And if your decision is already made, I will respect it completely.

This is my truth.
The rest is not in my control.

— From someone who cared deeply
